> KEYWORD.RESEARCH_

LONG-TAIL.KEYWORDS

Generate 30 long-tail keywords (3-5 words) for [YOUR TOPIC] targeting [YOUR AUDIENCE]. Focus on keywords with commercial or informational intent that would have low to medium competition. For each keyword, estimate the search intent.

SEARCH.INTENT.ANALYSIS

Analyze the search intent for these keywords: [LIST YOUR KEYWORDS]. For each keyword, specify if it's informational, navigational, transactional, or commercial investigation. Explain reasoning and suggest best content type.

QUESTION.KEYWORDS

What are the top 30 questions people ask about [YOUR TOPIC]? Organize by question type (what, how, why, when, where, who). For each, indicate difficulty level and content length needed.

> CONTENT.CREATION_

BLOG.OUTLINE

Create comprehensive blog outline for "[YOUR TITLE]" targeting [YOUR AUDIENCE]. Include: 1) Introduction with hook, 2) 6-8 H2 sections with H3 subsections, 3) Key points for each section, 4) Strong conclusion with CTA. Optimize for keyword "[YOUR KEYWORD]". Target: [LENGTH] words.

EMAIL.SUBJECT.LINES

Write 15 email subject lines for [CAMPAIGN TYPE] promoting [YOUR OFFER]. Target: [YOUR AUDIENCE]. Include: 5 question-based, 5 benefit-focused, 5 curiosity-driven. Keep under 50 characters for mobile.

SOCIAL.MEDIA.POSTS

Create 7 [PLATFORM] posts about [YOUR TOPIC] for [YOUR INDUSTRY] professionals. Each: [WORD COUNT] words, attention-grabbing hook, actionable value, engagement question. Vary formats: tips, stories, insights, how-tos. Include relevant hashtags.

GOOGLE.ADS.COPY

Write Google Ads copy for [YOUR PRODUCT/SERVICE]. Create: 15 headlines (30 chars max), 10 descriptions (90 chars max). Target keyword: "[YOUR KEYWORD]". Highlight: [YOUR BENEFIT]. Include pricing, USP, clear CTA, urgency elements.

LANDING.PAGE.COPY

Write conversion-focused landing page copy for [YOUR PRODUCT/SERVICE]. Include: 1) Hero headline & subheadline, 2) Problem statement ([PAIN POINTS]), 3) Solution presentation, 4) 5-7 key features with benefits, 5) Social proof, 6) FAQ (top 5 objections), 7) Strong CTA. Tone: [YOUR VOICE].

> UX.ANALYSIS_

COMPREHENSIVE.UX.AUDIT

Analyze this screenshot for usability issues. Evaluate: 1) Navigation clarity, 2) Visual hierarchy, 3) Accessibility (WCAG 2.1 AA), 4) Mobile responsiveness, 5) CTA effectiveness, 6) Form design, 7) Content readability. For each issue: severity rating, example, actionable recommendation. Prioritize by impact.

CONVERSION.OPTIMIZATION

Review this landing page for conversion optimization. Identify: 1) Friction points, 2) Missing trust signals, 3) CTA effectiveness, 4) Above-fold clarity, 5) Form issues. Prioritize by potential CR impact. Suggest A/B test ideas for top 3 improvements.

HEURISTIC.EVALUATION

Evaluate this interface against Nielsen's 10 usability heuristics. For each: 1) Rating (0-4 severity), 2) 2-3 specific examples, 3) Improvement recommendations. Cover all 10 heuristics systematically.

> STRATEGY_

CONTENT.GAP.ANALYSIS

Analyze my content strategy for [YOUR NICHE]. Current topics: [LIST YOUR TOPICS]. Competitor topics: [COMPETITOR TOPICS]. Identify: 1) Content gaps, 2) Trending topics I'm missing, 3) Question-based opportunities, 4) Depth gaps, 5) Format gaps. Create prioritized list of 20 content ideas.

AUDIENCE.ANALYSIS

Create detailed audience analysis for [YOUR PRODUCT/SERVICE]. Define: 1) Demographics, 2) Psychographics, 3) Pain points, 4) Goals, 5) Online behavior, 6) Buying triggers, 7) Preferred channels. Create 3 distinct buyer personas with backgrounds and use cases.
