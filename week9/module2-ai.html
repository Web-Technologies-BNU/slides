AI Prompts Library

Keyword Research:

"Generate 30 long-tail keywords (3-5 words) for [YOUR TOPIC] targeting [YOUR AUDIENCE]. Focus on keywords with commercial or informational intent that would have low to medium competition."

Blog Outline:

"Create a comprehensive blog post outline for '[YOUR TITLE]' targeting [YOUR AUDIENCE]. Include: 1) Introduction with hook, 2) 6-8 H2 sections with H3 subsections, 3) Key points for each section, 4) Strong conclusion with CTA. Optimize for keyword '[YOUR KEYWORD]'."

Social Media:

"Create 7 LinkedIn posts about [YOUR TOPIC] for [YOUR INDUSTRY] professionals. Each post: 150-200 words, include hook, provide value, end with engagement question. Vary formats: tips, stories, insights, how-tos."

Ad Copy:

"Write 15 Google Ads headlines (30 chars max) and 10 descriptions (90 chars max) for [YOUR PRODUCT]. Target keyword: '[YOUR KEYWORD]'. Highlight benefit: [YOUR BENEFIT]. Include strong CTAs."
