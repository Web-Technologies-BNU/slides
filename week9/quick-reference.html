Key Formulas

Conversion Rate

CR = (Conversions ÷ Total Visitors) × 100

Click-Through Rate

CTR = (Clicks ÷ Impressions) × 100

Return on Investment

ROI = ((Revenue - Cost) ÷ Cost) × 100

Customer Acquisition Cost

CAC = Total Marketing Spend ÷ New Customers
